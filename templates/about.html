Preserving History

The story of flavor, tradition, and three generations of pickling mastery.

Born in a Family Kitchen

HomeMade Pickles & Snacks began in a small kitchen with a big dream - to share the authentic flavors of Indian pickling traditions with the world. For three generations, our family has perfected the art of pickle-making using traditional recipes and natural ingredients.

We started by serving our local community, where the aroma of sun-dried mangoes and freshly ground spices became a hallmark of our neighborhood. Today, we bring those same handcrafted treasures to your doorstep.

The Art of Slowness

In an age of industrial manufacturing, we choose the long path. Our pickles are sun-ripened, hand-mixed, and age-matured in ceramic jars. This "slowness" is what allows the spices to penetrate deep into the fibers of the fruit, creating a complexity of flavor that machines simply can't replicate.

Our Core Philosophy

100% Natural

Sourced directly from local farmers with no artificial preservatives.

Handcrafted

Every jar is mixed and packed by hand in small, artisanal batches.

Traditional

Aged naturally under the sun using ancient Himalayan techniques.
